Privacy Policy for Scene Partner

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Scene Partner is an acting rehearsal tool developed by Bag Lunch Games. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data.

Information We Collect

Scene Partner does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data used by the app remains entirely on your device.

Microphone Access

Scene Partner requests access to your device's microphone solely to enable voice input features for rehearsal purposes. Audio is processed locally on your device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted to any server.

Text-to-Speech

Scene Partner uses your device's built-in text-to-speech engine to read script lines aloud. No text or audio data is sent to external servers.

Third-Party Services

Scene Partner does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Scene Partner does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
